# Privacy Policy for PromptEasy Extension

**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025

## Introduction

PromptEasy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you authenticate with the extension using an API token, we store your authentication credentials locally in your browser.
- **Prompts and Content**: Any prompts you create, save, or manage through the extension are stored on our servers and cached locally.
- **Usage Data**: We track basic usage statistics such as which prompts you use and when, to help you understand your prompt usage patterns.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Browser Information**: The extension detects which AI chat platform you're using (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) to provide appropriate functionality.
- **Error Logs**: Technical error information to help us debug and improve the extension.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the extension's functionality
- Sync your prompts across devices
- Improve prompt suggestions and AI-powered features
- Track usage analytics for your personal dashboard
- Debug and fix technical issues

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Authentication tokens, cached prompts, and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
- **Server Storage**: Your prompts, account information, and usage analytics are stored on our secure servers.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your prompts with other users (unless you explicitly make them public)
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Share your personal information with third parties except as required by law

## Third-Party Services

The extension connects to:
- **PromptEasy API** (prompteasy.ndsrf.com): For syncing prompts and account data
- **AI Services**: When you use the "Improve with AI" feature, your prompt content may be sent to AI services for processing

## Permissions

The extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

- **storage**: To cache prompts and settings locally
- **activeTab**: To interact with the current tab when inserting prompts
- **tabs**: To identify which AI platform you're using
- **Host Permissions**: To inject functionality into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, and Perplexity pages

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Authentication tokens are stored securely in Chrome's encrypted storage
- Access to your data requires valid authentication

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data through the extension and web app
- Export your prompts at any time
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt out of usage tracking

## Data Retention

- **Active Users**: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for 12+ months may be archived
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## Children's Privacy

The extension is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
